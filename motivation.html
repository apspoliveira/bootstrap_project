My name is António Oliveira and I am from Coimbra. I have a degree and a PhD in Computer Engineering from the University of Coimbra.

In the final work of Degree I improved software in the area of Software Engineering and Artificial intelligence. This work is part of a research project. During my PhD I was involved in the areas of affective computing and musical computing at the Center for Informatics and Systems of the University of Coimbra. I developed a system of music production according to emotions. This work resulted in a thesis of doctorate entitled "A Musical System for Emotional Expression".

I started my professional activity of teaching Higher Education in the academic year 2015/2016 at Instituto Superior Miguel Torga giving theoretical and practical classes in the discipline of Intelligence Artificial. In the 2016/2017 school year, I continued this activity at the Escola Superior de Technology and Management of Viseu giving practical classes in the disciplines of Programming Computers and Algorithms and Programming.

Since August 2017 I have been involved in computer programming. of August From 2017 to October 2017 I was working for the company G9Telecom from Coimbra, improving a Call Detail Records database and obtaining statistical measurements details about Call Detail Records. From December 2017 to February 2018 I collaborated in initial phase of a project exploring technologies and templates with the aim of development of an application for managing restaurant customer orders. At the period from September 2017 to September 2018, being linked to a co-work space from Instituto Pedro Nunes, I developed a web email client inspired by the web client made for Protonmail. From May 2019 to October 2019 I was a web programmer analyst at company Coimfor, being directed my work in the improvement and development for the GCIWeb application oriented to management and accounting. From October 2019 to February 2019 2021 I was a programmer at the company Let’s Call in which I was involved in three projects: importing invoices to the QuickBooks platform; collaboration in the development of SMSBUZZ application for sending SMS messages; collaboration in the development of Contact Center application used for the purpose of making telephone calls from a call center.

I have published some articles in international conferences and journals.
